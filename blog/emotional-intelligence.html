* What is Emotional Intelligence?

EQ stands for emotional quotient, defined as an individual's ability to sense, understand and effectively apply the power of these emotions to facilitate higher levels of collaboration and productivity.

* How is it different to IQ?

While IQ is a number to represent the relative intelligence of a person. While a person can be very intelligent/smart, this doesn't necessarily translate to collaboration and productivity, thus success (in different ways)

* Why is it important?

It helps with these things: successful leaders and superior performers have well developed emotional intelligence skills, as high EQ enables them to work well with a wide variety of people and to respond effectively to the rapidly changing conditions in the business world. In fact, a person's emotional intelligence may be a better predictor of performance success than intelligence.
