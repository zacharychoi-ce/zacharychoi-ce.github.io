* Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.
One of the struggles that I've had during University project work was, the lack of contribution of team members.
I have done several projects and I had different approaches in some of them. When no one was contributing I have done most of the work. Of course I have informed the lecturer about the contribution so that it would be fair - however it was not healthy for my time management and mentality.
Another project I would schedule / divide workload with percentages and gave each other different tasks - this worked best and proved to be useful. Thus giving everyone a goal / expectation to meet worked well.
